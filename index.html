Frequently Asked Questions


The profit generated by the treasury investments and from the platforms (launchpads, NFT marketplace and many more) is distributed among the stakers. Staking ensures that you will receive rewards in every profit distribution cycle.

For example, treasury made a profit of 100k dollars last month, and the community decides to keep 50% of the total profit in the treasury and keep it growing and the remaining 50% to be distributed. In that case, at the end of the month, 50k will be the total reward. This will be distributed to all sORCL holders. The amount each staker gets will depend on how much ORCL they staked and the time that they were in the protocol in the respective cycle.

Oracle protocol makes sure that the bonding price is always greater than the TAV (total assets backing of an ORCL token). The premium that is charged on each bond sale increases the TAV per ORCL. In simple terms, bonding increases the backing per ORCL, making it more valuable. Also, whenever ORCL is minted then it is backed by the TAV which gives it an intrinsic value.
